About BrainLeeds

Explore what we do at BrainLeeds and how you can get involved

🏆 Worsley Awards 2023

BrainLeeds is proud to have been recognised with the prestigious Best Committee prize at the esteemed Worsley Awards 2023. This achievement underscores our unwavering commitment to excellence in promoting the fascinating realm of clinical neuroscience among medical students.

Our dedication has translated into remarkable accomplishments, including successful fundraising initiatives that have enabled us to drive impactful events, such as Give-It-A-Go's that have garnered impressive traction from medical students at Leeds, encouraging them to get involved with our society's activities.

We take pride in our role as facilitators of knowledge and connection.Through our proactive efforts, we're helping bridge the gap between medical students and doctors and surgeons. We have run fundraisers for local charities, created an extensive network of doctors and surgeons, and embraced online teaching to empower the next generation of medical minds.

This recognition reaffirms our dedication to advancing the field of neurology and neuroscience, and we look forward to continuing our mission of inspiring and empowering medical students through enriching experiences and innovative initiatives.

🎓 Educate

Navigating neuroanatomy and clinical neuroscience is tough for medical students. We simplify complex subjects, offering accessible resources and expert guidance. BrainLeeds helps students grasp essential concepts, build a strong foundation, and excel in their medical education and practice.

🦠 Teach

The landscape of neurological disorders often presents with elusive symptoms. At BrainLeeds, we bridge this gap by equipping medical students with the knowledge and skills needed to decipher, recognize, and respond effectively to a wide range of common conditions.

👩🏼‍⚕️ Connect

We facilitate connections between medical students and neurologists and neurosurgeons, offering unique opportunities for networking and gaining firsthand insights. BrainLeeds empowers students to engage in meaningful dialogue, seek guidance, and absorb real-world knowledge.

💰 Fundraise

BrainLeeds actively seeks opportunities to raise funds that empower advancements in neurological health, utilizing creative initiatives, engaging events, and a dedicated community to drive meaningful impact.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Awareness

We passionately strives to raise awareness about neurological conditions, employing educational programs, interactive workshops, and impactful campaigns to foster understanding, empathy, and support within our community and beyond.

🏥 Exposure

BrainLeeds provides crucial firsthand exposure to this dynamic realm. Serving as a gateway, we offer insights into the intricate world of these disciplines, fostering a deeper understanding of their challenges and responsibilities through practical content and immersive experiences.
