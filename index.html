About Us

We are the University of Leeds Neurology and Neurosurgery Society! Established in 2021, our aim is to foster a strong interest in the clinical neurosciences amongst medical students. The field of neurology is an ever-changing landscape filled with cutting-edge science.

Aims

  • Making neuroanatomy and clinical neurosciences easier to understand
  • Providing more exposure to the field and what it entails
  • Providing a better chance to connect with local neurologists and neurosurgeons
  • Understanding how to recognise and respond to common neurological conditions
